## About Me

20-year-old Frontend Developer specializing in **Next.js**, **TypeScript**, and **Solana blockchain development**.

**Started my crypto journey in 2017 at age 13**, diving deep into blockchain tech and Web3. Been through all the cycles - bull markets, bear markets, and everything in between. Now focusing on building tools that actually solve problems in the space.

### My Coding Journey
- **Started learning to code in late 2023** - Self-taught through building real projects
- **Building practical Web3 tools** that bring value to the crypto community
- **Bridging AI and blockchain technology** with modern frontend development
- **Performance obsessed** - <10ms execution times, 90+ PageSpeed scores

When not coding, I enjoy gaming and water skiing. Born with a congenital hand difference, which has shaped my perspective on accessible design and inclusive technology.
